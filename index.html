About

The Symposium

DigiFest 2026 is a one-day student symposium celebrating technology, creativity, and collaboration — where ideas ignite.

DigiFest 2026 brings together the brightest student minds for a day of intense competition, creative exploration, and technological discovery. From cutting-edge coding battles to wildly creative non-technical events, every participant will leave inspired.

Our symposium is built on four pillars that define the future of student innovation — where technology meets imagination.

🚀
Innovation
Break boundaries and think beyond conventional solutions.
🎨
Creativity
Express ideas through code, design, and imagination.
💻
Technology
Harness modern tools to solve real-world problems.
🤝
Collaboration
Grow together through teamwork and shared knowledge.
